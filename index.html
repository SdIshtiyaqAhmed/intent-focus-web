Features

Quiet tools for deep work.

Focus Scopes

Build lightweight profiles for coding, writing, design, or anything else you want to protect.

Smart Interventions

Local Analytics

See where your attention actually went, with session history stored on your own machine.

Privacy by Default

Why It Works

Less force. More awareness.

Intent is built around interruption with purpose. It creates just enough space between impulse and action for you to choose deliberately.
